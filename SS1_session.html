IoT Based Grid Integration of Renewable Energy System and its Challenges (SS1)

Session Chair

M. Venkateshkumar

Chairman IEEE YP Madras Section and Associate Professor, Dept of EEE| AVIT, Chennai

Area of Content:
The main objective of this special session is to motivate and create the bridge in between the young researchers, Students, Academicians and entrepreneur. To share their knowledge advance technologies in renewable Energy systems and develop innovations in renewable energy system using advance technologies such as IoT, Smart and Intelligent Controllers.
The topics of interest include, but are not limited to :
• IoT based Renewable Energy System (RES)
• MPPT Controllers
• Grid Integration of RES
• Power Quality Improvements
• Innovative Inverters
• Smart Grid
• Microgrid
• Battery Energy Management system
